Meet Samantha

I never set out to become a consultant. I set out to build businesses. And that's exactly what I did for over twenty years.

I took over operations of Cashland Holdings, LLC and Borrow Money Now from my father, who founded the business back in 1987. Over the next two decades, I grew the company from a single location to ten retail stores across Oklahoma and evolved our products from traditional payday lending to a revolving line of credit model, something I helped pioneer in the industry.

In 2010, I saw the shift toward digital and launched our online lending platform, now known as Borrow Money Now. By 2013 it had real momentum, and I stayed deeply involved in every part of the operation, from managing the call center to shaping customer experience.

In 2025, I successfully sold the business to a larger multi-state operator. It was bittersweet, but it was time. And it gave me the chance to do something I'd been thinking about for years: take everything I learned building my own companies and help other business owners apply it to theirs.

That's how Elevate was born.
