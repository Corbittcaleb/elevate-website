Services

How I Can Help

Every business has its own set of challenges. Here's where I tend to make the biggest impact. Some clients need all of this. Most start with one or two things and build from there. Either way, we figure it out together.

Operational Optimization

If something in your business feels harder than it should be, it probably is. I come in, map out how things actually work today, and find where time, money, and energy are slipping through the cracks. Then we build SOPs and KPIs that make consistent performance the default, not the exception. You end up with a business that runs better whether you're in the room or not.

Quality Enhancement

Your reputation is built one customer interaction at a time, and small inconsistencies add up faster than most owners realize. I help you define what a great experience looks like at your business, then build the training, feedback loops, and accountability systems to make it repeatable. From mystery shopping programs to service audits, we measure what matters and fix what needs fixing before it becomes a bigger problem.

Project Implementation

Some projects are too important to let drift. A product launch, a system rollout, a process overhaul. These things need someone to own them from start to finish, not just advise from the sidelines. I step in as the person responsible for keeping it on schedule, on scope, and on budget, and I don't disappear when things get complicated. I stay in it until it's done.

Employee Recognition

Good people leave when they feel invisible. I've managed teams large and small, and I know the difference recognition makes when it's intentional versus when it's an afterthought. I help you design programs that actually mean something to your team, along with satisfaction surveys that surface problems while there's still time to address them. Happy employees build better businesses. It really is that straightforward.

Administrative Support

The behind-the-scenes work that keeps a business compliant and organized is easy to deprioritize until it creates a problem. Annual filings, state requirements, compliance deadlines, event planning, training logistics. I handle all of it so nothing falls through the cracks while you focus on running your business. Think of it as having a detail-oriented partner who actually enjoys this kind of work.

Vendor Cost Analysis

Most companies are overpaying for at least a few things, and they have no idea. I review your vendor contracts, benchmark your current rates against what's available, and negotiate better terms or find competitive alternatives. The savings I find typically cover the cost of working together, and then some. It's one of those services where the upside is usually much bigger than business owners expect going in.

B2B Introductions

The right introduction at the right moment can open doors that years of cold outreach never would. Through my work with the Entrepreneurs' Organization and two decades of building relationships in Oklahoma and beyond, I've developed a network worth tapping into. When I connect two businesses, I do it thoughtfully, because my reputation depends on the quality of every introduction I make.

Business & Personal Gifting

A well-chosen gift says a lot about the kind of company you are. Whether it's a closing gift for a client, birthday recognition for a team member, or a thoughtful thank-you for a long-time partner, I handle everything from selection to delivery. No generic gift cards. No forgettable swag. Just gifts that feel personal and reflect well on you, without taking up any of your time.

The Process

How It Works

No complicated onboarding. No long-term contracts required before we know if it's a fit. Just a simple path from conversation to results.

1

Schedule a Consultation

We start with a real conversation, just the two of us. You tell me what's going on in your business, what's working, what's not, and where you feel stuck. I ask questions and actually listen. No sales pitch, no pressure.

2

Get a Custom Plan

Based on what I learn, I put together a plan built around your specific situation. Not a templated package, not a one-size-fits-all recommendation. Something that actually makes sense for where you are and where you want to go.

3

See Results

I work alongside your team to make things happen, not just hand you a document and wish you luck. We track progress, adjust as needed, and keep moving until the work produces something you can actually measure and feel.

Not sure where to start? That's completely fine.

Most of my best client relationships started with someone saying, "I just know something needs to change." That's enough. Let's figure out the rest together.
